Peak Distric National Park

The Peak District National Park is the UK’s original national park. The Peak District National Park has three main landscapes, the Dark Peak, White Peak and South West Peak. Each has it's own character and sense of place.

Lake District National Park

The Lake District National Park is England's largest National Park. It is home to 10 mountains, 16 lakes, hidden tarns and around 28,500 hectares of woodland.

Snowdonia National Park

Situated on the west coast of Britain covering 823 square miles of diverse landscapes, Snowdonia National Park boasts the highest mountain in England and Wales, and the largest natural lake in Wales.

Dartmoor National Park

Along with the ponies, one of Dartmoor’s most iconic sights is its tors. The national park has more than 160 tors; dramatic outcrops of granite, usually standing on top of a hill.
